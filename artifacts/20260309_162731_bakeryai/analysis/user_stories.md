## US-001 - Manage Product Catalog

**Persona:** Bakery Staff  
**Goal:** Add, edit, and delete bakery products in the system  
**Business Benefit:** Allows for accurate and up-to-date product information  
**Priority:** High

### Acceptance Criteria
- As a bakery staff member, I can add a new product to the catalog with all necessary details
- As a bakery staff member, I can edit an existing product's details
- As a bakery staff member, I can delete a product from the catalog

## US-003 - Track Inventory of Raw Materials

**Persona:** Bakery Staff  
**Goal:** Monitor and manage inventory levels of raw materials  
**Business Benefit:** Prevents stockouts and minimizes wastage  
**Priority:** Medium

### Acceptance Criteria
- As a bakery staff member, I can view the current inventory levels of raw materials
- As a bakery staff member, I can receive alerts when inventory levels reach a certain threshold
