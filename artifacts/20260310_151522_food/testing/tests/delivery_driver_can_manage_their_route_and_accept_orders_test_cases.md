## Test Design for US-003 - Delivery driver can manage their route and accept orders

**Target Module:** `delivery_driver_can_manage_their_route_and_accept_orders`

### Acceptance Criteria Under Test
- Driver can view their current route and upcoming orders
- Driver can accept or decline orders
- Driver can navigate to the delivery location using the app
- Driver can update order status in real-time
- Driver can communicate with the user regarding estimated delivery time

### Test Scope
- Unit validation for generated module import and behavior.
- Output contract validation for dictionary-based response shape.
- Smoke-level integration readiness for SDLC pipeline.
