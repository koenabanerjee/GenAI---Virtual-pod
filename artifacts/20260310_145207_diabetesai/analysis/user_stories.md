## US-001 - Diabetes Prediction System: Patient Data Collection

**Persona:** Healthcare Professional  
**Goal:** Collect and input patient data for diabetes prediction  
**Business Benefit:** Improve accuracy of diabetes prediction  
**Priority:** High

### Acceptance Criteria
- Can input patient demographic information (age, gender, ethnicity)
- Can input patient medical history (family history, past diseases)
- Can input patient lifestyle data (diet, exercise, smoking)
- Can input patient lab results (glucose levels, HbA1c, cholesterol)

## US-002 - Diabetes Prediction System: Predict Diabetes Risk

**Persona:** Patient  
**Goal:** Receive diabetes risk prediction based on input data  
**Business Benefit:** Empower patients to take preventative measures  
**Priority:** Medium

### Acceptance Criteria
- System accurately predicts diabetes risk based on input data
- Patient receives clear and understandable diabetes risk assessment
- Patient can view historical diabetes risk assessments

## US-003 - Diabetes Prediction System: Integration with EHR

**Persona:** Healthcare Professional  
**Goal:** Access diabetes risk predictions within Electronic Health Record  
**Business Benefit:** Improve patient care and treatment planning  
**Priority:** High

### Acceptance Criteria
- System integrates with Electronic Health Record (EHR)
- Healthcare professional can view diabetes risk predictions for patients
- Healthcare professional can access historical diabetes risk assessments
