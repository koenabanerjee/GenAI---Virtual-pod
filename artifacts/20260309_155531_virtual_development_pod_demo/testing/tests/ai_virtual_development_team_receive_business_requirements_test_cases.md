# AI Virtual Development Team - Receive Business Requirements

## 1. Objective
The objective of this test case is to verify that the AI system in the `ai_virtual_development_team_receive_business_requirements` module can accurately interpret and understand business requirements, and identify key information and extract relevant details from them.

## 2. Preconditions
- The AI system is properly installed and configured.
- The AI system has access to the business requirements document.
- The business requirements document is clear, concise, and complete.

## 3. Test Cases

### Test Case 1: Accurately Interpret Business Requirements
| Test Case ID | Test Description | Expected Result |
| --- | --- | --- |
| TC001 | Provide a simple business requirement to the AI system. | The AI system should accurately interpret the business requirement. |
| TC002 | Provide a complex business requirement to the AI system. | The AI system should accurately interpret the business requirement, even if it contains multiple parts or conditions. |
| TC003 | Provide a business requirement with ambiguous or unclear language. | The AI system should ask for clarification or provide an error message indicating the ambiguity or unclear language. |

### Test Case 2: Identify Key Information
| Test Case ID | Test Description | Expected Result |
| --- | --- | --- |
| TC004 | Provide a business requirement with multiple key information. | The AI system should correctly identify all key information. |
| TC005 | Provide a business requirement with missing key information. | The AI system should identify the missing key information and request it from the user. |
| TC006 | Provide a business requirement with incorrect key information. | The AI system should identify the incorrect key information and request it to be corrected. |

### Test Case 3: Extract Relevant Details
| Test Case ID | Test Description | Expected Result |
| --- | --- | --- |
| TC007 | Provide a business requirement with multiple relevant details. | The AI system should correctly extract all relevant details. |
| TC008 | Provide a business requirement with irrelevant details. | The AI system should ignore irrelevant details and focus on the relevant ones. |
| TC009 | Provide a business requirement with missing relevant details. | The AI system should identify the missing relevant details and request them from the user. |

## 4. Exit Criteria
- The AI system accurately interprets all business requirements.
- The AI system correctly identifies all key information.
- The AI system extracts all relevant details from the business requirements.
