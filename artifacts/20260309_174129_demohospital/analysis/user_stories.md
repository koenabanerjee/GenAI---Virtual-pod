## US-001 - Centralized Patient Record Management

**Persona:** Hospital Administrator  
**Goal:** Manage and maintain patient records in a centralized system  
**Business Benefit:** Reduce manual paperwork and improve efficiency in managing patient records  
**Priority:** High

### Acceptance Criteria
- As a hospital administrator, I want to be able to add, update, and delete patient records
- As a hospital administrator, I want to be able to search for patient records using various search criteria
- As a hospital administrator, I want to be able to grant access to patient records to authorized personnel

## US-002 - Appointment Scheduling and Doctor Availability

**Persona:** Hospital Staff  
**Goal:** Schedule appointments and manage doctor availability  
**Business Benefit:** Improve efficiency by reducing manual scheduling and providing quick access to doctor availability  
**Priority:** Medium

### Acceptance Criteria
- As a hospital staff member, I want to be able to schedule appointments for patients
- As a hospital staff member, I want to be able to view doctor availability in real-time
- As a hospital staff member, I want to be able to send appointment reminders to patients

## US-003 - Billing and Medical History Management

**Persona:** Hospital Billing Department  
**Goal:** Manage billing and medical history for patients  
**Business Benefit:** Reduce manual paperwork and provide quick access to patient medical history for billing and insurance purposes  
**Priority:** High

### Acceptance Criteria
- As a hospital billing department member, I want to be able to generate invoices for patients
- As a hospital billing department member, I want to be able to view patient medical history for billing purposes
- As a hospital billing department member, I want to be able to update patient insurance information
