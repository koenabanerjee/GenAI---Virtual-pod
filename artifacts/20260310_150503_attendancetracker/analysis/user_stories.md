## US-001 - Student Attendance Marking

**Persona:** Teacher  
**Goal:** Mark attendance for students in a class  
**Business Benefit:** Accurately track student attendance  
**Priority:** High

### Acceptance Criteria
- As a teacher, I can mark attendance for all students in a class
- Attendance marks are saved and persist in the system
- Attendance reports can be generated for a specific class and date range

## US-002 - Student Attendance Viewing

**Persona:** Student  
**Goal:** View their attendance records  
**Business Benefit:** Students can monitor their attendance and take action if necessary  
**Priority:** Medium

### Acceptance Criteria
- Students can view their attendance records for the current semester
- Students can view their attendance records for previous semesters
- Students can download or print their attendance records

## US-003 - Student Attendance Notifications

**Persona:** Parent/Guardian  
**Goal:** Receive notifications about student attendance  
**Business Benefit:** Parents/guardians are informed of attendance issues  
**Priority:** Medium

### Acceptance Criteria
- Parents/guardians receive notifications when a student has excessive absences
- Parents/guardians can view their child's attendance records online
- Parents/guardians can receive attendance notifications via email or SMS
