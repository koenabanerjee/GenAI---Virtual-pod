## US-001 - As a teacher, I want to be able to input student data for analysis

**Persona:** Teacher  
**Goal:** Input student data for academic performance prediction  
**Business Benefit:** Identify students who may need additional support and provide early intervention  
**Priority:** High

### Acceptance Criteria
- Teacher can input student data such as attendance, assignment scores, exam marks, and participation
- Data can be saved and retrieved for analysis

## US-002 - As a teacher, I want to view student performance reports

**Persona:** Teacher  
**Goal:** View student performance reports for academic performance prediction  
**Business Benefit:** Identify trends and insights to provide early intervention and improved learning outcomes  
**Priority:** Medium

### Acceptance Criteria
- Teacher can view reports with student performance data
- Reports can be filtered by student, date range, and performance metric

## US-003 - As a teacher, I want to receive notifications for students at risk

**Persona:** Teacher  
**Goal:** Receive notifications for students with low academic performance  
**Business Benefit:** Provide early intervention and improved learning outcomes  
**Priority:** High

### Acceptance Criteria
- Teacher receives notifications for students with low academic performance
- Notifications can be configured to preferred frequency and communication channel
